Python

Python is one of the most beginner friendly languages in the world, and while there are a ridiculous amount of beginner tutorials, there are less guides on intermediate to advanced Python, especially on the internals of how the compiler CPython works. The links below assume a basic level of understanding of Python

  • Fluent Python, 2nd Edition(Walkthrough of most of the advanced Python user level features)

  • Deep Dive into Python(Udemy, Paid, intermediate)
